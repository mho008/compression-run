2XU SINGAPORE 2019

7 APRIL 2019, F1 PIT BUILDING

For the morning birds who get excited at the idea of waking up at the crack of dawn to race down the streets of Singapore without a hefty price tag, 2XU Compression Run is the run for you. With a flag-off time at 5am at the F1 pit building and a running route through the city, this run has 5km, 10km and 21.1km categories to choose from. All participants will receive a 2XU running singlet and finisher medal.
